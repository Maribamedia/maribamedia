About Mariba Media

Mariba Media is a dynamic technology media holding company, dedicated to driving innovation and growth across multiple critical sectors. Our mission is to connect partners, clients, and stakeholders with transformative solutions.

Our Mission

To empower businesses and communities through innovative solutions in energy, water, technology, skills development, commodities, and investments, fostering sustainable growth and prosperity.

Our Vision

To be the leading technology media holding company in Africa, recognized for our excellence in delivering integrated solutions that create value for our stakeholders and contribute to the continent's development.

Our Objectives

  • Provide innovative and sustainable solutions across all our sectors
  • Build strategic partnerships with industry leaders and stakeholders
  • Empower communities through skills development and capacity building
  • Drive economic growth through responsible investment practices
  • Establish Mariba Media as a trusted brand in the African market
