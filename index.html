How It Works

We are an introducing broker. Our only job is to pass your submission to a network of licensed funders. Nothing more, nothing less.

1

Submit Your Project

Fill the secure form with your project details and attach standard documents. No assessment, no advice.

2

We Forward to the Network

Your submission is transmitted unchanged to all regulated lenders in our network. No filtering, no selection.

3

A Lender Contacts You Directly

If interested, a funder reaches out to you. All discussions, terms, and due diligence are between you and them.

4

We Only Earn on Success

Our introducing commission is paid by the lender, not by you, and only when a facility is deployed. You never pay us.
