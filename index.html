Frequently Asked Questions

What type of invoices qualify for discounting?

Invoices issued to verified government departments, municipalities, or other public sector entities for goods or services that have already been delivered.

Does the work need to be fully completed before funding?

Yes, the goods must be delivered or the services rendered before the applicable invoice can be discounted.

How much of the invoice value can typically be advanced?

We typically advance up to 70% of the invoice value upfront, with the balance paid once the debtor settles the invoice.

How is repayment structured?

The government debtor pays the invoice, and the proceeds are used to settle the funding provided.

How long until funds are paid?

Typically, funds are available within 3–5 days from submission of complete documentation.
