Debt, Equity & Blended Finance

Our network includes institutional investors, DFIs and infrastructure funds that provide specialist capital for fully developed energy projects. When you submit your IPP project, your documentation is forwarded directly to these funders. They will conduct their own due diligence and contact you if there is a fit. No curation, no matching — just a clean introduction.

Standard Documents Funders Typically Require

  • • Executive Summary & Site Location
  • • PPA structure, EIA status, technical design
  • • Municipal approval, grid connection feasibility
  • • Financial model (CAPEX, OPEX, IRR, DSCR)
  • • Off‑taker credit assessment or sovereign guarantee
  • • Consortium team organogram & project schedule

All lending and investment decisions are made exclusively by the funder. Mariba Media does not participate in any credit decision or provide advice.
